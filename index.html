    Kendra Fortmeyer is the fiction editor for Broad! magazine and a Pushcart Prize nominee with an MFA from the New Writers Project at UT Austin. Her work has appeared or is forthcoming in lots of sexy places. She wrote a YA magical realist novel about girls and their bodies that's found brilliant representation at The Bent Agency and that is looking for, as we say, a forever home.

    When she's not writing, she teaches the world's craziest, most brilliant teenagers at Skybridge Academy in Austin, Texas.

    You get in touch at kendra.fortmeyer [at] gmail[dot]com, or hear her jibber-jabber on Twitter @kendraffe ...which is what her name would be if she were a giraffe.*
