Kendra Fortmeyer is the fiction editor for Broad! magazine and a Pushcart Prize nominee with an MFA from the New Writers Project at UT Austin. Her work has appeared or is forthcoming in the Toast, PANK, Smoking Glue Gun, NANO Fiction, apt, Juked, Forge, Fiddleblack, Corium and elsewhere. She wrote a YA magical realist novel about girls and their bodies that's found brilliant representation at The Bent Agency and is looking for, as we say, a forever home.

When she's not writing, she teaches the world's craziest, most brilliant teenagers at Skybridge Academy in Austin, Texas.

You can hear her jibber-jabber on Twitter @kendraffe ...which is what her name would be if she were a giraffe.*
